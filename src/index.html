Welcome to Ocean Breeze Guest

Experience the magic of Sri Lanka's stunning coastline at our beautifully appointed beachfront apartment in Hikkaduwa. Just a 2-minute walk from pristine golden sands, our property offers the perfect blend of tropical charm and modern comfort.

Whether you're here for surfing, snorkeling at the famous coral reefs, exploring the historic Galle Fort, or simply relaxing with the sound of waves, Ocean Breeze Guest is your ideal home base for an unforgettable Sri Lankan adventure.

Our spacious 2-bedroom apartment comfortably accommodates up to 6 guests, featuring a fully equipped kitchen, high-speed WiFi, air conditioning, and a private balcony with stunning ocean views.
